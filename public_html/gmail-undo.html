Want to cancel an email?

Gmail labs now have a smart solution for you!

  • Step 1

    Click on tools button in the upper right corner

  • Step 2

    Select settings

  • Step 3

    Click on labs tab
    1. Type undo in the search field
    2. Click enable
    3. Click save settings
    You will now be sent back to your inbox

  • Step 4

    In the settings tab make sure that undo send is enabled. You can now choose the time you want availible for cancelation 5 - 30 sec in the drop down field.

  • Step 5

    Remember to save your changes

  • PS! Gmail Labs is a testing ground for experimental features that aren't quite ready for primetime. They may change, break or disappear at any time.
