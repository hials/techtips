How to clear your screen

Have more control over your work.

Tweet

Sondre Fjærtoft

Sondre@fjærtoft.com

Published 25.03.2015 at 10:41

Do you have a lot of windows open at the same time? Do you only need one? Here is a simple trick to help you solve that problem. Click the top of your window, hold down the mouse and drag it from side to side with alittle speed. Every other window but the one you are dragging is now minimized .

But fear not, if you need your other windows back up, simply do the same once more.
